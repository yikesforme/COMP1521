1. jal func (func has address 5096)
	-> 5096 / 4096 = 1.
	-> loading address space "Code2" which is 1 in Physical Memory.
2. lw $s0, ($sp) ($sp points to address 28668)
	-> 28668 / 4096 = 6.999
	-> loading address space "Stack2" which is 2 in Physical Memory.
3. la $t0, msg (msg is at 10192)
	-> 10192 / 4096 = 2.488
	-> loading address space "Data" which is 6 in Physical Memory.
